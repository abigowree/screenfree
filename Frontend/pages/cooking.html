Cooking

medium ⏱ 30 min

About This Activity

Prepare a healthy meal while focusing on the process of cooking mindfully without distractions.

Benefits:

  • Improves Lif skills
  • Encourages healthy eating
  • Boosts focus and patience

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Gather all ingredients and utensils
2
Wash and prepare vegetables or ingredients
3
Cook slowly while focusing on each step
4
Serve and enjoy the meal mindfully

Tips for Success:

Keep your phone away while cooking. Focus on flavors, smells, and movements to fully enjoy a screen-free experience.
