Morning Reading Session

easy ⏱ 30 min

About This Activity

Start your day with an inspiring book or article to stimulate your mind.

Benefits:

  • Improves focus
  • Reduces stress
  • Increases knowledge

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Choose a quiet, comfortable reading spot
2
Select a book or article that interests you
3
Read for 30 minutes without distractions
4
Take notes of interesting points

Tips for Success:

Remember to keep your phone away during this activity. Focus entirely on the present moment and enjoy the experience of being screen-free!
