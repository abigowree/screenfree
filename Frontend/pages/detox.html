Detox

high ⏱ 45 min

About This Activity

Digital detox helps reduce screen dependency by taking a break from phones, social media, and gadgets.

Benefits:

  • Reduces screen addiction
  • Improves concentration and calmness
  • Enhances real-world interaction

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Enhances real-world interaction
2
Choose an offline activity (reading, walking, journaling)
3
Spend time mindfully without checking screens
4
Reflect on how you feel after the detox session

Tips for Success:

Keep all digital devices out of reach. Be present in the moment and enjoy screen-free time.
