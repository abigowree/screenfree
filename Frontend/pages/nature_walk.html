Nature Walk

easy ⏱ 30 min

About This Activity

Start your day with an inspiring book or article to stimulate your mind.

Benefits:

  • Boosts mood
  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Vitamin D exposure

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Choose a safe walking route
2
Walk at a comfortable pace
3
Observe your surroundings mindfully
4
Take deep breaths of fresh air

Tips for Success:

Remember to keep your phone away during this activity. Focus entirely on the present moment and enjoy the experience of being screen-free!
